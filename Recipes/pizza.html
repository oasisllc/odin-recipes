Pizza

Pizza is a delicious Italian dish that is made with a crust, tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It is a great meal to make for a special occasion or just for a cozy night in. Here is a simple recipe for pizza that you can try at home:

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup of sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup of sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup of sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  3. Place dough on a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread tomato sauce over dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over sauce.
  6. Top with pepperoni, olives, mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions.
  7. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over toppings.
  8. Drizzle olive oil over pizza.
  9. Season with oregano, basil, and garlic powder.
  10. Bake in preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  11. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
