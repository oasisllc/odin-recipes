Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish that is made with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce. It is a great meal to make for a special occasion or just for a cozy night in. Here is a simple recipe for lasagna that you can try at home:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef until brown. Add onions and garlic, and cook until onions are transparent. Stir in tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes. Season with basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 20 minutes.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, Parmesan cheese, and 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  4. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch pan. Layer with uncooked lasagna noodles, cheese mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Continue layering until all ingredients are used, reserving 1/2 cup mozzarella. Cover pan with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
