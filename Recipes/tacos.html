Tacos

Tacos are a delicious Mexican dish that is made with a crispy or soft shell, seasoned meat, and various toppings. They are a great meal to make for a special occasion or just for a cozy night in. Here is a simple recipe for tacos that you can try at home:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 12 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef until brown. Add onions and cook until onions are transparent. Stir in taco seasoning and refried beans. Heat until warm.
  2. Heat taco shells according to package instructions.
  3. Fill taco shells with meat mixture, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, sour cream, and cilantro.
  4. Enjoy!
